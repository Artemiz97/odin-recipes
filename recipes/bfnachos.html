Breakfast Nachos

Breakfast Nachos

are a savory and satisfying twist on the traditional Tex-Mex favorite, tailored for the morning meal. They typically start with a base of crispy tortilla chips layered with breakfast staples like scrambled eggs, melted cheese, and crumbled bacon or sausage. Common toppings include avocado slices, black beans, jalapeños, and a drizzle of sour cream or salsa, making them a flavorful, hearty option to kick-start the day.

This dish is popular for its versatility and ease of preparation — perfect for a weekend brunch or even a creative breakfast-for-dinner choice. Whether baked in the oven or assembled fresh, breakfast nachos offer a fun and shareable way to enjoy a variety of bold flavors and textures in one dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips (about 150 – 200g)
  • 3–4 eggs, scrambled
  • 3–4 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 to 1½ cups shredded cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a cheese blend
  • 1 avocado, sliced or diced
  • ¼ cup black beans (cooked or canned, rinsed)
  • 1 jalapeño, thinly sliced (optional)
  • ¼ cup cherry tomatoes, halved or diced
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons salsa or hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the bacon or sausage until browned and crisp.
  3. Remove and set aside on a paper towel.
  4. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Pour into the same skillet and cook over medium-low heat until just set.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. On a baking tray or oven-safe dish, spread out a layer of tortilla chips.
  8. Add scrambled eggs, crumbled bacon/sausage, and shredded cheese evenly on top.
  9. Bake for 5–7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and top with avocado, black beans, jalapeños, tomatoes, and cilantro.
  11. Drizzle with sour cream and salsa or serve them on the side.
  12. Serve immediately while hot and crispy.
